Space marines

Defenders of humanity in the 41st millenium

What are Space marines?
Space marines are genetically modified warriors that serve as mankind's most powerful army. They were created by the Emperor of Mankind as His most powerful army. First he created the 20 Primarchs, true superhumans uncomparable to humans like you or me. The Gods of Chaos stole the infantile primarchs, scattering them accross the galaxy - some say because he used knowledge stolen from them to create the Primarchs - and so the Emperor began his Great Crusade to find his lost sons, and reunite humanity. To this end, he created the 20 legions of Space Marines with some leftover genetic material from one of the Primarchs, so that he could give each Primarch an army of his own.

The legio astartes
The Legio Astartes were large groups of Space Marines that varied wildly in size. They were the way the Space Marine forces were organized from when they were first created until the Horus Heresy in M34 (M34 means the 34th millenium).
